Colegio de Kidapawan

Colegio de Kidapawan (CdK), formerly known as the North Cotabato Institute of Technology (NORCIT), is a private, non-sectarian tertiary institution located on Quezon Boulevard in Kidapawan City, Cotabato, Philippines. Established in 1986, CdK began with a modest team of instructors and staff, operating initially from a rented building on Datu Ingkal Street. In 1992, the college relocated to its current campus along Quezon Boulevard. Over the years, CdK has expanded its academic offerings to include various technical courses and four-year degree programs, such as Bachelor of Science in Nursing, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Hotel and Restaurant Management, Secondary Education, Elementary Education, Mass Communication, Criminology, and Business Administration. All degree programs are duly recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).
